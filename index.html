This is a record and review of dining at Gaggan, Asia's No.1 and world's No.5 restaurant ranked by The World's 50 Best Restaurants. The creative 25-course emoji menu is a culinary journey, taking around 3 hours to finish. The wine pairing menu consists of 8 glasses of wine and 1 glass of Japanese sake.

The Establishment

Located in Bangkok, Gaggan is a fine-dining restaurant offering progressive Indian dishes.

  1. The name : Gaggan is established by and named after the famous Indian chef Gaggan Anand in 2010.
  2. The chef : Born in Kolkata, India, Gaggan Anand is an award-winning chef with great passion for music, who finds a unique way to combine and utilize the music with the cuisine.
  3. The house : Set in a stunning whitewashed colonial-style wooden house in the heart of downtown Bangkok, the michelin restaurant Gaggan offers a culinary experience like no other.
